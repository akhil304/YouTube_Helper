📱 Import from Any Device

Watch YouTube on your phone, tablet, smart TV? No problem. Google keeps all your history in one place. Export it with Google Takeout and import it here — all devices, one history.

Step 1 — Download Your Data
1
Go to Google Takeout
Visit takeout.google.com — sign in with the same Google account you use for YouTube
2
Select only YouTube & YouTube Music
Click "Deselect all" then tick only ✅ YouTube and YouTube Music to keep the file small
3
Choose JSON format
Click "Multiple formats" → set History format to JSON (not HTML)
4
Export & Download
Click "Next step" → "Export once" → Google emails you a .zip file (usually within minutes)
5
Extract & Import
Unzip → find Takeout/YouTube and YouTube Music/history/watch-history.json → drag it below
Step 2 — Drop Your File
📂
Drop watch-history.json here
or click to browse — works on mobile too
File stays on your device, never uploaded anywhere
